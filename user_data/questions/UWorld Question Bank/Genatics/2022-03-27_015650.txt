(Choice C) Pulsatile GnRH infusion can stimulate ovulation in women who have disordered

Educational objective:

In vitro fertilization using a donated ovum is the most promising means of achieving pregnancy
in a woman with Turner syndrome. Pregnancy can occasionally occur spontaneously in some
patients with Turner syndrome, but the risk of spontaneous abortion, Down syndrome and
Turner syndrome are all increased in such cases.
