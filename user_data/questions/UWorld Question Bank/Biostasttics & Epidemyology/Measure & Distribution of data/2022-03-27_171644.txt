(Choices D and E) In general, in a negatively skewed distribution (with the "tail" on the left),
the mean is the most shifted in the negative direction, followed by the median and then the
mode.

Educational objective:

In general, in a positively skewed distribution, the mean is the most shifted in the positive
direction (to the right), followed by the median and then the mode. In such a situation, the
median often reflects a central tendency better than the mean does.
