analgesics on pain alleviation in patients with idiopathic small fiber neuropathy. A total of 100
eligible patients who fulfilled the inclusion/exclusion criteria are assigned to one of two groups
based on random selection by a computer. The method of assigning patients to treatment

groups in this study is most likely intended to produce which of the following?
