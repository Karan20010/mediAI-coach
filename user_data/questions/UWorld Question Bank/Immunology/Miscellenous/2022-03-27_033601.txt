(Choice E) Plasma cells are the principal cells responsible for the synthesis of
immunoglobulins, which are directly responsible for type II and III hypersensitivity reactions.
Educational objective:

Poison ivy dermatitis is a form of allergic contact dermatitis, which is a type IV hypersensitivity

reaction mediated primarily by T lymphocytes. It manifests as intensely pruritic erythematous
papules, vesicles, or bullae that often form linear patterns.
