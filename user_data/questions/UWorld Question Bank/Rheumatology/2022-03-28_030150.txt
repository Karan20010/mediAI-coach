(Choice E) This structure is the gluteus maximus muscle. This muscle is innervated by the
inferior gluteal nerve and is the major extensor of the thigh at the hip.

Educational Objective:

The Valsalva maneuver increases vagal tone and can be used to abolish paroxysmal
supraventricular tachycardia. The rectus abdominis is the most important muscle in achieving
the increased intraabdominal and intrathoracic pressure of the Valsalva maneuver.
