Educational objective:

The Thayer-Martin medium is used to isolate pathogenic Neisseria species such as N
meningitidis and N gonorrhoeae. \t is a chocolate sheep blood agar that contains vancomycin
to inhibit the growth of gram-positive organisms; colistin and trimethoprim to inhibit gram-
negative bacteria (other than pathogenic Neisseria); and nystatin to inhibit yeasts.
