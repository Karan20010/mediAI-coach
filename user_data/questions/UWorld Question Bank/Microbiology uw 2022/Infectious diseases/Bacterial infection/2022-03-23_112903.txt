(Choice F) Nucleotide and non-nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitors block viral reverse
transcription. Although acute retroviral syndrome (early HIV infection) can present with a
diffuse maculopapular rash and fever, patients also often have diffuse lymphadenopathy, a
sore throat, and painful oral ulcers. In addition, RMSF is more likely in this case given this
patient's recent camping trip.
