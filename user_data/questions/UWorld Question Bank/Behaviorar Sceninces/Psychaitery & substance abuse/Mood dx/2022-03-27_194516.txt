Educational objective:

Postpartum mood disturbances include postpartum blues, postpartum depression, and, rarely,
postpartum psychosis. The most common disturbance, postpartum blues, is a benign, self-
limited condition that begins several days postpartum and resolves within 14 days without
intervention.
