Educational objective:

Bulimia nervosa (BN) is characterized by recurrent episodes of binge eating followed by
compensatory behaviors to prevent weight gain (eg, fasting, exercising, vomiting, using
laxatives). In contrast to patients with anorexia nervosa, BN patients are normal weight to
overweight.
