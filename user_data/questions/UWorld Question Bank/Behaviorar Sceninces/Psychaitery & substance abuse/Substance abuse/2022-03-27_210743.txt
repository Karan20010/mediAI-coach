Educational objective:

Marijuana contains tetrahydrocannabinol, which stimulates cannabinoid receptors to produce a
mild euphoria with inappropriate laughter, increased appetite, slowed reaction time/motor
speed, and cognitive impairment. Tachycardia and conjunctival injection are the 2 most
immediate physical symptoms of marijuana use.
