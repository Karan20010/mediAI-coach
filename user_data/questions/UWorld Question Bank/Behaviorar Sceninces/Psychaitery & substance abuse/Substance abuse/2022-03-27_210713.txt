Educational objective:

Inhalant intoxication is characterized by immediate onset of euphoria, lethargy, ataxia, and/or
loss of consciousness followed by rapid recovery within 45 minutes. Perioral and perinasal
dermatitis (ie, "glue sniffer's rash") may be seen in chronic users.
