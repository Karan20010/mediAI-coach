Educational objective:

Frontotemporal dementia presents with early personality change, executive dysfunction,
compulsivity, and hyperorality. Neuropathologic findings include neurofibrillary tangles due to
abnormal tau proteins (also seen in Alzheimer dementia) and pathologically ubiquitinated TDP-
43 (also seen in amyotrophic lateral sclerosis).
