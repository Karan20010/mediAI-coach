Protooncogenes/tumor promoters

Antioncogenes/tumor suppressors

ABL: chronic myeloid leukemia, acute lymphoblastic leukemia

APC/p-catenin: stomach cancer, colon cancer, pancreatic
cancer, familial adenomatous polyposis

BRAF: melanoma

BRCA1, BRCA2: breast cancer, ovarian cancer

HER1: squamous cell lung cancer

DCC: colon cancer

HERz2: breast cancer, ovarian cancer

NF1: neuroblastoma, neurofibromatosis type 1, sarcoma

MYC: neuroblastoma (NMYC); small cell lung cancer (LMYC)

RB: retinoblastoma, osteosarcoma, other cancers

RAS: lung cancer, colon cancer, pancreatic cancer (KRAS); renal
cell cancer, bladder cancer (HRAS)

TP53: most cancers, Li-Fraumeni syndrome

S/S: astrocytoma, osteosarcoma

VHL: Von Hippel-Lindau syndrome, renal cell cancer

TGFA: astrocytoma, hepatocellular carcinoma

WT1: Wilms tumor
