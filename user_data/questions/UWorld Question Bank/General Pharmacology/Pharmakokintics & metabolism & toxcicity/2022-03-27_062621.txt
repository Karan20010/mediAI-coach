(Choice C) Leucovorin, or folinic acid, is a drug used in the treatment of methotrexate

Educational objective:

Hemorrhagic cystitis during therapy with cyclophosphamide or ifosfamide is caused by the

urinary excretion of the toxic metabolite acrolein. It can be prevented by aggressive hydration,
bladder irrigation, and administration of mesna, a sulfhydryl compound that binds acrolein in

the urine.
