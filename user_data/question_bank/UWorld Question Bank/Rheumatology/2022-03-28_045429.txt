Educational objective:

Sjégren syndrome is an autoimmune disorder characterized by lymphocytic inflammation in
exocrine glands (eg, lacrimal, salivary). It presents with dry eyes (keratoconjunctivitis sicca)
and dry mouth (xerostomia). Chronic B-cell proliferation increases the risk for non-Hodgkin

lymphoma.
