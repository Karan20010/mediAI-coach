(Choice E) Chronic glucocorticoid use is a major risk factor for osteoporosis. Glucocorticoids
decrease the gastrointestinal absorption of calcium, inhibit collagen synthesis by osteoblasts,
decrease GnRH (leading to hypogonadism), and increase urinary calcium loss.

Educational objective:

Regular exercise leads to increased peak bone mass, a reduced rate of bone loss, anda
decreased risk of osteoporosis.
