Educational objective:

Marfan syndrome is due to a defect in fibrillin-1, an extracellular glycoprotein that acts as a
scaffold for elastin. It is abundant in the zonular fibers of the lens, periosteum, and aortic
media. Aortic root dilation with dissection and rupture is a common cause of death.
