Educational objective:

Systemic sclerosis (SS) is an autoimmune disease characterized by the widespread fibrosis of
multiple organ systems. Histology demonstrates expansion of the dermal layer with diffuse
collagen deposition and atrophy of the intradermal adipose tissue and dermal appendages.
