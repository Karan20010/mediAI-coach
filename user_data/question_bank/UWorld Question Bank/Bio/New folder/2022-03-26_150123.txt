Educational objective:

Vitamin B,. deficiency often presents with megaloblastic anemia (impaired DNA synthesis) and
neurologic deficits (impaired myelin synthesis). Characteristic neurologic findings include
subacute combined degeneration of the dorsal columns and lateral corticospinal tract.
Elevations in methylmalonic acid and homocysteine levels occur due to decreased metabolism
of these molecules.
