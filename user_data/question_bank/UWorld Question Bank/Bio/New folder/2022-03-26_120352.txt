(Choice C) Pantothenic acid (vitamin B,) is a component of coenzyme A, which is a cofactor

(Choice D) Riboflavin (vitamin B,) is the precursor for flavin adenine dinucleotide (FAD)

Educational objective:

Niacin (vitamin B,) is the precursor for NAD and NADP. Niacin toxicity leads to increased
prostaglandin production, which causes cutaneous vasodilation, flushing, and pruritus that may
be avoided by pretreating with nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs.
