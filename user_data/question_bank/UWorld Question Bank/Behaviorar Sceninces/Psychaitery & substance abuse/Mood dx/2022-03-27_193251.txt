Manic episode

Clinical features

¢ 21 week of elevated or irritable mood & increased
energy/activity

23 of the following symptoms (4 if mood is irritable only)
(DIGFAST mnemonic):

© Distractibility

© Impulsivity/indiscretion, risky behavior

o Grandiosity

© Flight of ideas/racing thoughts

© Increased activity/psychomotor agitation

o Decreased need for sleep

© Talkativeness/pressured speech

Severity

¢ Impaired psychosocial function
e May have psychotic features (hallucinations, delusions)
e May require hospitalization
