(Choice E) Trazodone is a highly sedating antidepressant that can be helpful for patients with
insomnia. Trazodone can cause sexual dysfunction and priapism, a rare but serious side
effect.

(Choice F) Venlafaxine is a serotonin-norepinephrine reuptake inhibitor that can cause sexual
side effects.

Educational objective:

Sexual dysfunction is seen in up to 50% of patients treated with selective serotonin reuptake
inhibitors. Bupropion, a norepinephrine-dopamine reuptake inhibitor, is a first-line treatment for
major depressive disorder and does not cause sexual dysfunction.
