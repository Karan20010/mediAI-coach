(Choices D, E, and F) Seizures, tachycardia, and tremors are features of alcohol and
benzodiazepine withdrawal syndromes. These features may be seen in stimulant overdose
but not in stimulant withdrawal.

Educational objective:

Stimulant withdrawal is characterized by dysphoric mood, fatigue, hypersomnia, hyperphagia,
and vivid dreams. In contrast to many other withdrawal syndromes, stimulant withdrawal is not
associated with significant findings on physical examination.
