Anorexia nervosa & bulimia nervosa

Anorexia
nervosa

Significantly low weight

Intense fears of gaining weight/distorted body image
Subtypes:

© Binge/purge

© Restricting (dieting, fasting, excessive exercise)

Bulimia nervosa

Weight within or above normal range

¢ Preoccupation with weight & shape

Binge eating & compensatory behavior (purging, exercise, fasting,
laxatives)
