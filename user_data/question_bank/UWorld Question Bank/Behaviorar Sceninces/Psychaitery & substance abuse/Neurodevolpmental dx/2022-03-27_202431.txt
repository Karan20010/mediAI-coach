Tourette syndrome

Clinical
features

¢ Both multiple motor tics & 21
vocal tics
(not necessarily concurrent) >1
year
© Motor: facial grimacing,
blinking, head/neck jerking,
shoulder shrugging, tongue
protrusion, sniffing
© Vocal: grunting, snorting, throat
clearing, barking, yelling,
coprolalia (obscenities)
¢ Onset age <18 (commonly age 6-
15)
