(Choice E) Reassurance alone is rarely effective in managing somatic symptom disorder.
Optimal management consists of regularly scheduled visits so that the patient does not need

Educational objective:

Somatic symptom disorder is best managed with regularly scheduled medical visits that are
not contingent on having active symptoms. Unnecessary diagnostic testing and specialist
referrals should be avoided.
