(Choice E) p53 is a tumor suppressor that causes cell cycle arrest and apoptosis. It is
rendered ineffective in the majority of cancers.

Educational objective:

Calcineurin is an essential protein in the activation of interleukin-2, which promotes the growth
and differentiation of T cells. Immunosuppressants such as cyclosporine and tacrolimus work
by inhibiting calcineurin activation.
