Anaphylaxis

Triggers

Immune mediated (eg, medications, food, insect stings)
Nonimmune mediated (eg, intravenous contrast, mast cell activation
syndrome)

Clinical
manifestations

Cardiovascular

© Vasodilation — hypotension & tissue edema
© Tachycardia

Respiratory

o Upper airway edema — stridor & hoarseness
° Bronchospasm — wheezing

Cutaneous

© Urticarial rash, pruritus, flushing
Gastrointestinal

o Nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain

Treatment

Intramuscular epinephrine
