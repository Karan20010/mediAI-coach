Ocular: periorbital swelling, injected conjunctiva, tearing

Cutaneous/ |e Oral mucosa: swelling, tingling, or itching of tongue & lips; metallic
mucosal taste
¢ Skin: urticaria, flushing, pruritus
¢ Nose: congestion, rhinorrhea
Respiratory {+ Upper airway: edema — hoarseness & stridor

Lower airway: bronchospasm — wheezing, dyspnea, tachypnea

Cardiovascular

Gastrointestinal

Nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, diarrhea
