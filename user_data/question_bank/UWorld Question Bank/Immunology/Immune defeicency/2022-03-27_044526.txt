(Choices C and E) Eosinophils and fibroblasts are not involved in type IV hypersensitivity
reactions. Eosinophils are part of the innate immune system and are increased with atopic or
parasitic diseases. Fibroblasts are found in connective tissue and are integral in wound
healing through the production of extracellular matrix and collagen.

Educational objective:

The Candida antigen skin test assesses the activity of cell-mediated immunity because it
requires an intact CD4 cell response. Failure to respond to candida antigen testing is typical in
patients with severe combined immunodeficiency.
