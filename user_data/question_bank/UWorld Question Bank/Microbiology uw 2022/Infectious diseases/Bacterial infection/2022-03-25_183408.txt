Suppurative parotitis in adults

Risk factors

Decreased salivary flow

¢ Medications (eg, anticholinergics)

¢ Obstruction (eg, calculi, neoplasm)
¢ Dehydration, postsurgical/intubation

Microbiology

¢ Staphylococcus aureus, anaerobes

Clinical
presentation

e Firm, erythematous pre-/postauricular swelling
¢ Trismus, dysphagia, systemic findings (fever, chills)

Diagnosis

¢ Imaging (ultrasound/CT scan): ductal inflammation/obstruction,
frank abscess
¢ Laboratory: elevated serum amylase without pancreatitis
