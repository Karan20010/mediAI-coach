Educational objective:

Giardia lamblia is the most common enteric parasite in the United States and Canada and is a
common cause of diarrhea in campers/hikers. lodine-stained stool smear classically shows
pear-shaped, flagellated trophozoites or ellipsoidal cysts with smooth, well-defined walls and
2+ nuclei. Metronidazole is the treatment of choice.
