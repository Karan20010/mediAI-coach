Educational objective:

Enterobius vermicularis infection (enterobiasis) occurs most frequently in children and
presents with perianal pruritus. Diagnosis is made by identifying eggs in the perianal region
(cellulose tape test). Treatment options include albendazole, mebendazole, and pyrantel
pamoate.
