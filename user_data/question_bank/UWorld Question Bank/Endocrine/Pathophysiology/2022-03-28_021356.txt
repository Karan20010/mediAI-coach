TH = thyroid hormone; TPO = thyroid peroxidase antibody; TRAb = thyrotropin receptor antibody;

TSI = thyroid-stimulating immunoglobulin.

This patient has postpartum thyroiditis (PPT) presenting with hyperthyroidism (ie, elevated
thyroid hormone levels, suppressed TSH) and a diffuse, nontender goiter. The clinical
course typically has 3 phases: hyperthyroid phase (1-3 months) due to release of preformed
thyroid hormone, hypothyroid phase (4-8 months) due to depletion of thyroid hormone stores,
and recovery phase with return to euthyroid state. Not all 3 phases occur in all patients.
