Serotonin syndrome

¢ Serotonergic medications, especially in combination (eg, SSRI/SNRI,
TCA, tramadol)

Causes ¢ Drug interactions: Serotonergic medication & MAOI or linezolid

¢ Intentional overdose of serotonergic medications

¢ Serotonergic drugs of abuse (eg, MDMA)

¢ Mental status changes (eg, anxiety, agitation, delirium)

Clinical ¢ Autonomic dysregulation (eg, diaphoresis, hypertension, tachycardia,
features hyperthermia, vomiting, diarrhea)

¢ Neuromuscular hyperactivity (eg, tremor, myoclonus, hyperreflexia)

MAOI = monoamine oxidase inhibitor, MDMA = 3,4-Methylenedioxymethamphetamine;
SNRI = serotonin-norepinephrine reuptake inhibitor; SSRI = selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor;

TCA = tricyclic antidepressant.
