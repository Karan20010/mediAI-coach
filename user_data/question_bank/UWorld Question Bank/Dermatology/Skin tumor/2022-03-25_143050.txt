Kaposi sarcoma

Etiology

Endothelial tumor caused by human
herpesvirus type 8

Most common malignancy in patients
with untreated HIV

Endemic in certain African &
Mediterranean regions

Manifestations

Cutaneous lesions:

o Violaceous, red, or brown flat
lesions — papules

©o Most common along skin folds

© Pruritus, pain & friability are rare

o Lymphedema distal to lesions

Visceral lesions:

© Lungs & gastrointestinal tract most
common

© May cause life-threatening bleeding
