Candida chorioretinitis & endophthalmitis

Epidemiology

Transient/persistent fungemia — hematogenous dissemination to the
choroid — invasion of the retina & fluid chambers

¢ Risk factors: indwelling central catheter, TPN, broad-spectrum

antibiotic use, gastrointestinal surgery & immunocompromise

Manifestations

Usually unilateral floaters & progressive vision loss
Pain rare until late in disease process
Fever may be absent & patient may otherwise feel well

Diagnosis

Blood cultures (often negative)
Vitreous fluid sampling & culture
