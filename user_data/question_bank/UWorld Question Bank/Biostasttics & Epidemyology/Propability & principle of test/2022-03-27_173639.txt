Question 32

The positive predictive value (PPV) of a diagnostic test answers the following question:
Given a positive test result, what is the probability that a patient has the disease? PPV
corresponds to the number of people with the disease who test positive among all those who
test positive. Using a standard contingency (2 x 2) table, PPV = a/ (a+b).
